# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 21, 2026**

BeSolaire ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Bequill mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- **Google Account Information**: When you sign in with Google, we collect your name, email address, and profile photo as provided by Google OAuth.

### 1.2 Information We Collect Automatically
- **Usage Data**: Sentences you write for practice, session history, scores, and feedback received.
- **Device Information**: Device type and operating system version for app functionality.

### 1.3 Information Collected by Third Parties
- **Google AdMob**: We use AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve relevant ads. See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **Firebase**: We use Firebase for authentication, database, and analytics. See [Firebase Privacy Policy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy).

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:
- Provide and maintain the App
- Authenticate your account via Google Sign-In
- Store your practice sessions and progress
- Display relevant advertisements via Google AdMob
- Improve the App's features and user experience
- Respond to your support requests

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services. All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS. We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your personal information.

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:
- **Google Firebase**: For authentication and data storage
- **Google AdMob**: For advertising purposes
- **Google Gemini API**: Sentences you submit for analysis are processed by Google's AI services. We do not store these on third-party servers beyond what is necessary for the service.

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

---

## 6. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your account and data
- Withdraw consent at any time

---

## 7. Account Deletion

You can delete your account and all associated data directly from the App:

1. Open Bequill
2. Tap your profile icon
3. Go to **Settings**
4. Tap **Delete Account**

All your data including session history, saved items, and profile information will be permanently deleted.

Alternatively, you can request account deletion by contacting us at **bbsoftwarecontact@gmail.com**.

---

## 8. Children's Privacy

Our App is intended for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

---

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
